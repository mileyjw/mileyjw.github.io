MY START IN MEDIA

I wanted to share some of the past work I have done and why it has given me so much joy! I have loved graphic design for a long time, but my actual start point of creating work was in high school.

My last semester of my senior year was the first time in school history that we got to have a graphic design class. That year I took initiative and started to create graphics for our sports teams...

Before that, two things had stopped me from creating graphics in the past:

  • I didn't have the time to go to all the sporting events and take photos.
  • My high school self did not want to be the "weirdo" taking unauthorized photos of my peers.

So, yearbook class gave me the authority and inspiration to start...

Reflecting back has made me realize how significant this experience was for me.
